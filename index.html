books. for most of my life the practice of reading has felt far out of reach. that changed in early 2021. i committed to a few strategies with which i have found some success in my efforts to read.

each of these books has influenced my formation like sea waves shaping a stone. some i may get to read again. their presence here reminds me how i got to wherever it is that i am.

↓

some strategies i employ to not suck at reading:

i stopped asking myself, “when am i gonna read?” and started asking, “when am i successful at reading?” now i only read when those conditions are met. if i don’t feel up for it, i am ok abandoning the task and moving on.

i allow myself to experience a book however i am capable of in a moment. often that is transiently. i work against the pressure to “make progress” or to “be an expert.” i can always read a book again. i find letting a work influence me in the moments i have with it to be a more substantive experience than aiming to be a scholar about it.

i try to read things that challenge my status quo. things that don't “expand my mind” as much as they change it. staying present in the discomfort of the unfamiliar allows me to be more open to and affected by others. this purpose has made the act of reading easier for me.
